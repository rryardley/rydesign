About Me

Hi there! Thanks for dropping by.

I know how much time and effort goes into running a business. I also know how frustrating it can be to spend hours on something outside your area of expertise, only to have it come out less than perfect. And if that something is your website you could be missing out on a prime opportunity to showcase your business. That’s where I come in. I can help you create a website and logo that reflect and enhance your unique brand, so you can focus on your business. Spend your valuable time doing the parts of your business that you love! Let me take care of sharing it with the world.

I'm an engineer with an eye for design.

I am a Web Designer and Developer with a background in engineering and art. I graduated from Cornell University with a BS and MEng in Engineering in 2014. I have taken art classes through highschool, college, and post-grad life. I love designing and developing websites because it combines my love of logic and problem-solving with my passion for art and design. I enjoy working with clients who want to highlight and enhance their unique brand through their website. If that’s you, please get in touch!
