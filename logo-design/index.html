Logo Designs Based On Consumer Psychology Research

A logo is more than just a symbol to use in marketing materials - it is the face of your brand and the first impression for your business. Something this important needs to be designed intentionally and thoughtfully to not only represent your unique brand, but to evoke emotion and build connection between your patrons and your business.


I conduct consumer research before ever touching pen to paper when designing logos. I use consumer psychology research to choose colors, fonts, and styling that align and connect with your mission, your brand personality, and your clientele.
